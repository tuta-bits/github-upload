Big Data is the latest buzzword. Everything that you see around you thrives on data, and its facets. Every action that you take, expenditure you make, and the income you receive, all get filed into a massive database, accessible by the ones who wish to analyse the same. These structured or unstructured data are often mined, extracted, and utilised in different forms, in order to rationalise decisions and devise algorithms that would enhance human life.

Cybersecurity - cracking the code


The Cybersecurity Web

In the wake of several cyber security attacks, including large scale DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service) Attacks, cybersecurity is a major concern in the virtual world we live in today. These attacks target information that fall under various domains, and could even result in a severe identity theft and impersonation, while accessing highly sensitive records. That brings us to the question - How secure are we online? With identity essentials such as banking having moved online, we are constantly in a flux of moving across cyber borders. This provides attackers motive and fodder to knock down your online presence. It is thus, highly important to adhere to certain rules and regulations while staying online, in order to remain safe and limit your exposure to the lowest possible extent.
